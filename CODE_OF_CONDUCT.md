# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors, maintainers, and community members pledge to make participation in A11y-Spy a respectful and inclusive experience for everyone.

A11y-Spy is an accessibility-focused project, so we especially value empathy, patience, and inclusive communication.

## Expected Behavior

Examples of positive behavior include:

- Being respectful of different opinions and experiences.
- Giving constructive feedback.
- Helping newcomers understand the project.
- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.
- Being patient when explaining accessibility concepts.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Intentionally disruptive comments.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Dismissing accessibility needs or disability-related concerns.
- Other conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional community.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, or ban contributors who violate this Code of Conduct.
